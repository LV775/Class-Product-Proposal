What is the product?

Application that makes it easy to maintain the health of your plants

1. Name: PlantPal

2. Team members: Just me

3. Application Overview: This application makes it easy to manage and maintain the health of your plants by integrated watering reminders and other cool features. The application also can track the amount of sunlight your plant is getting.

4. Technology Overview:

5. Feature list: The application can set watering reminders, can track the amount of sunlight your plants are getting. The application can tell you what products you should buy for your plants health. It can also give you access to an extensive plant database and give you suggestions on which plants to plant based on ur soil conditions and amount of sunlight available.

6.Future feature list: In the future there could be a community interaction group that could talk online about their plants, give suggestions toothers and share pictures of their plants to the group.

7. Basic Technical Features: Users can upload a picture of their plants and the application will diagnose the health and provide feedback on things you can do to make your plants come to life more.
